# CatFriends: community and adoptions

## Context
Feline associations need digital tools to manage adoptions and communities.

## Problem
- Fragmented and poorly accessible platforms 
- Lack of tracking for adoptions 
- No integrated community tools 

## Approach
- Stakeholder analysis (volunteers, adopters, associations) 
- Prototyping with shared wireframes 
- Continuous testing on accessibility 

## Solution
- Responsive web platform with multi-role 
- Management of listings, cat profiles, adoption tracking 
- Moderated internal forum and chat 

## Results
- Over 500 adoptions facilitated 
- Increased community engagement 
- WCAG 2.1 AA certified accessibility 

## Conclusion
CatFriends is a concrete example of technology serving social needs, with a real impact on adoptions.
