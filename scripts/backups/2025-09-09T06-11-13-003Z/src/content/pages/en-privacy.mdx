# Privacy Policy

This policy describes the methods of processing personal data of users who consult and use the site **chermaz.com** (hereinafter, the "Site").

## Data Controller

**Stefano Chermaz** - Data Controller for the data collected through the Site. For requests or exercise of rights, use the [Contact](/it/contact/) page.

## Types of Data Processed

- **Browsing Data**: IP address, date/time, requested URL, user-agent, and technical data necessary for the functioning of the Site.
- **Data Provided by the User**: content sent via contact forms or other channels indicated on the Site.
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies**: technical cookies and, if present, third-party cookies. For more details, refer to the "Cookies" section.

## Purposes and Legal Bases

- **Functioning and Security of the Site** (legal basis: *legitimate interest* of the Data Controller).
- **Response to Requests** sent via the Site (legal basis: *execution of pre-contractual measures* or *contract*).
- **Legal Obligations** (legal basis: *legal obligation*).
- **Anonymous Statistical Activities** where implemented (legal basis: *legitimate interest* or *consent* if required).

## Methods of Processing and Retention Periods

Data is processed using IT tools and appropriate security measures. Retention periods are commensurate with the purposes: contact data is retained for the time necessary to respond to and manage the request; technical logs for security for a limited period compatible with the purposes of protection and defense.

## Recipients and Providers

Data may be processed by providers delivering technical services (e.g., hosting, maintenance, communication tools) appointed as *Data Processors* pursuant to Article 28 GDPR. An updated list of providers is available upon request.

## Transfers Outside the EU

If certain services involve transfers to non-EU countries, these will occur in compliance with Chapter V of the GDPR (e.g., adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, or other adequate guarantees).

## Rights of the Data Subject

You can exercise at any time the rights provided for in Articles 15-22 GDPR (access, rectification, deletion, limitation, portability, objection, withdrawal of consent) by contacting the Data Controller via the [Contact](/it/contact/) page. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent Supervisory Authority.

## Cookies

The Site uses technical cookies necessary for its functioning. Any third-party or profiling cookies will only be activated with prior consent, if present. You can manage your browser preferences to limit or delete cookies. For details on the types and available choices, refer to your browser settings and any consent banner, if displayed.

## Minors

The Site is not intended for minors under 14 years of age. If data of minors are inadvertently collected, the Data Controller will delete them upon request.
