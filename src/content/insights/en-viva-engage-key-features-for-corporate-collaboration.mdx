# Viva Engage: key features for business collaboration

If you are looking for an innovative way to enhance business collaboration within your organization, then Viva Engage (originally Yammer) might be the solution you are seeking! Discover all the incredible features of this new tool and transform the way you work with your colleagues. Read this article to learn more!



## Introduction to Viva Engage and its Importance in Business Collaboration

Introduction to Viva Engage (formerly Yammer) and its importance in business collaboration



Communication is a fundamental element for the success of any business activity. Good communication within the organization allows employees to work more efficiently, share ideas, and achieve common goals. However, there are often obstacles that make effective communication difficult between team members or across different departments.



For this reason, Microsoft has developed Viva Engage, a new tool dedicated to corporate collaboration. Thanks to its multiple features, Viva Engage helps organizations overcome the challenges of internal communication and create a more productive and cohesive work environment.



What exactly is Viva Engage? It is a digital platform integrated into Microsoft Teams, the application for managing daily business activities. With Viva Engage, you can access a range of features that promote collaboration and employee engagement.



One of the main features of Viva Engage is the ability to create thematic groups where employees can share information, documents, and resources relevant to their work. This promotes transparency within the company and allows team members to stay updated on what is happening in the rest of the organization.



Viva Engage offers an instant messaging system that facilitates communication among colleagues, eliminating the need to use email or other external platforms. This allows for quicker responses and a more efficient management of requests and inquiries.



Another key feature of Viva Engage is the ability to conduct polls and votes. Thanks to these options, employees can express their opinions on specific topics or make decisions quickly and democratically. This promotes employee engagement and allows them to feel involved in the company's choices.



Finally, but not least, Viva Engage also offers a dedicated space for training and knowledge sharing within the organization. Employees can access educational and training resources, share their experiences, and learn from one another. This promotes a culture of continuous learning within the company and helps employees develop new skills.



In summary, Viva Engage is a fundamental tool for corporate collaboration as it facilitates communication, promotes employee engagement, and fosters knowledge sharing within the organization. Thanks to this platform, companies can enhance the efficiency and productivity of their employees, creating a more cohesive and collaborative work environment.



## Key Features of Viva Engage:

The main features of Viva Engage are designed to help companies improve communication and collaboration among employees, enabling them to work more efficiently and productively.



The first feature we want to present to you is the activity feed. This tool allows employees to share updates, news, or questions with colleagues in real time. The feed can be used to notify about ongoing projects, request feedback on an idea, or simply to stay updated on the latest company news.



Another important feature is represented by workgroups. With Viva Engage, it is possible to create groups dedicated to specific projects or topics, where members can share documents, ideas, and discuss relevant issues. This promotes greater collaboration among employees and facilitates the exchange of information within the organization.



Viva Engage also offers an integrated chat that allows employees to communicate instantly with each other. The chat is available for both one-to-one and group conversations and is a very useful tool for quickly resolving issues or clarifying doubts without having to send emails or organize meetings.



Thanks to the integrated platform Yammer, Viva Engage allows employees to participate in discussions on specific topics and share knowledge and ideas with colleagues from other departments or locations. This feature promotes greater collaboration among different teams and stimulates creativity within the company.



Another important feature of Viva Engage is the ability to conduct polls and surveys to gather feedback from employees. This allows managers to assess the performance of activities, employee preferences, and identify any areas for improvement.



Finally, Viva Engage offers a personalized dashboard where each employee can view their ongoing activities, updates from the groups they participate in, their goals, and much more. This tool allows employees to keep track of their work and maintain focus on the most important activities.



## &#8211; Real-time communication through chat

Real-time communication has become increasingly important in the work dynamics of modern companies. Thanks to technological evolution, it is now possible to interact with colleagues and collaborators immediately, wherever they may be.



With Viva Engage, this type of communication is made even simpler and more efficient thanks to the integrated chats within the platform. Chats allow for instant communication between team members, both one-on-one and in groups.



Through the Viva Engage chats, it is possible to exchange information and documents quickly and securely. Thanks to the voice messaging feature, you can record short audio clips to express thoughts or ideas even more quickly and effectively.



But that's not all: Viva Engage also offers the ability to create thematic channels within chats to better manage the various activities and projects of the company. Each channel can be customized with a specific name and description, and only the interested team members can access it.



Each channel has access to very useful collaboration tools, such as the ability to attach files or pin important notes directly in the conversation. This way, all team members can access the necessary information to work together effectively.



Another very useful feature of chats on Viva Engage is the ability to reply to a specific message without having to rewrite all the previous content. This way, communication is clearer and more organized, avoiding confusion among team members.



Finally, thanks to the ability to integrate chats with other apps and external services, such as Microsoft Teams or Google Calendar, it is possible to manage all business activities more effectively on a single platform.



In summary, the chat functionality of Viva Engage makes real-time communication even more effective and intuitive for better collaboration within the company. Make the most of this opportunity to enhance your teamwork and achieve increasingly satisfying results!



## – Document Sharing and Management

Document sharing and management are fundamental aspects of effective business collaboration. Thanks to Viva Engage, Microsoft's new collaboration tool, it will be possible to simplify and optimize these processes.



One of the main features of Viva Engage is indeed the ability to easily share documents within the team or the entire company. Simply upload the document to the platform and select the members you wish to share it with. This way, everyone will have access to the file in real-time and can work on it simultaneously, without the need for constant email exchanges or using different programs.



But that's not all: thanks to cloud-based technology, Viva Engage also allows simultaneous editing of documents by multiple users. This means that every team member can make their changes to the document in real-time, without the risk of overwriting or losing information. The system keeps track of previous versions of the files, ensuring greater control over their evolution.



In addition to simple document sharing, Viva Engage also offers advanced features for file management. You can create folders and subfolders to organize documents based on the projects or thematic areas they belong to. Furthermore, you can set specific permissions for each folder or file, determining who has the right to view, edit, or share the documents.



But that's not all: Viva Engage also offers a notification system that alerts users when changes are made to the documents they have access to. This way, every team member will always be aware of the latest updates and can quickly get up to speed.



Viva Engage allows for the integration of all major Microsoft Office tools, such as Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. This means that you can work on documents using the same applications and interface that we are accustomed to, but with the convenience of having everything stored and shared on a single platform.



In summary, thanks to the document sharing and management offered by Viva Engage, collaborating within the company becomes simpler and more efficient. Not only are work times reduced and errors and duplications avoided, but a more collaborative and integrated work environment is also created.



## – Workspaces and groups to organize projects

Thanks to its wide range of features, Viva Engage offers users the ability to organize their projects efficiently and collaboratively. Among the various options available, "Workspaces" and "Groups" stand out as essential tools for project management within the platform.



The "Workspaces" are areas dedicated to specific projects, where users can share information, documents, and resources in one place. This facilitates communication among team members and allows them to collaborate in real-time on the same document or task. Additionally, it is possible to invite both internal and external colleagues to participate in your workspace, promoting even broader collaboration.



Within the "Workspaces," it is possible to create subgroups to divide the project into phases or specific activities. This way, each team member can focus on their responsibilities without being distracted by other information that is not relevant at the moment. It is also possible to assign tasks to individual group members and track the status of progress in real time.



Another important feature offered by Viva Engage is the "Groups." These areas aim to promote dialogue and the exchange of ideas among all employees of the company. Each group has a specific theme, such as a department or a cross-functional project, and is accessible to all those who are part of it. This allows for a broader view of business activities and involves all employees in the development of initiatives.



The "Groups" can be used to share important news and updates regarding the company or to create discussions on specific topics. This feature promotes transparency and employee participation in company life, thereby increasing the sense of belonging and engagement in achieving common goals.



In summary, thanks to "Workspaces" and "Groups," Viva Engage provides users with a comprehensive tool to better organize their projects and promote more effective and transparent collaboration within the company.

2. Communicate with messages: Once you have created the groups, you can start communicating with your team through Viva Engage messages. You can send private messages to a single member or post one in the group to share important information or start a discussion.



3. Use the chat: In addition to messages, you can also use the chat function to communicate with your team in real-time. This is a great resource when working on urgent or complex projects that require immediate communication.



4. Share files: Thanks to the built-in storage feature, you can easily share files with your team on Viva Engage. You no longer have to worry about bulky emails or losing important files across different platforms. You can set file access permissions to control who can view or edit them.
