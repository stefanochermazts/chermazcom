# chermaz.com: IT consulting showcase

## Context
Stefano Chermaz's personal site needed a redesign to reflect his consulting activities.

## Problem
- Previous site in WordPress was fragmented and unfocused  
- Uneven SEO (only positioned sudoku)  
- Lack of a clear value proposition

## Approach
- Personal positioning and keyword analysis  
- Choice of stack **Astro + Tailwind + Netlify** for performance and simplicity  
- Continuous testing on accessibility and SEO

## Solution
- New static site with Services, Case Studies, Insights sections  
- Migration of SEO-optimized articles  
- Redirect from WordPress and sudoku on dedicated subdomain

## Results
- Improved ranking on IT consulting keywords  
- Clearer UX for prospects and clients  
- Blog/LinkedIn integration for lead generation

## Conclusion
The new chermaz.com is now a professional showcase and an active tool for marketing and consulting.
